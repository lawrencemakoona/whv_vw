Employment skills and training

The Gentown Employment Skills and Training Program (GESTP) helps jobseekers become work-ready by providing pre-employment training. This program is supported by the local Chamber of Commerce and Industry members.

Our focus is to give individuals a better understanding of employers’ expectations in both the recruitment process and as new employees in the workplace. This means providing opportunities for jobseekers to develop and practise their IT, problem solving and communication abilities. We work with businesses to ensure that people are acquiring industry relevant skills and knowledge.

At GCBH, our aim is to assist Employment Skills and Training participants in achieving the best outcomes, whether they’re looking for long-term employment or guidance charting a career path. The GESTP program works closely with our business arm, Gentown Recruitment Services, to connect participants with local industries through work placements and mentorships.
