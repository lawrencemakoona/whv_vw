Mission, vision and values

Our mission:

To support community driven innovation for the well-being of all.

Our vision:

Inspired people working together to meet the needs of the whole community.

Our values:

  • Quality in all aspects of what we do.

  • Innovation in creating solutions that meet the needs of everyone in the community.

  •  Accountability in how we operate, by being clear on how and why we engage with business and community.

  • Collaboration with each other as we recognise that by working together, we can achieve better outcomes.

  • Respect for all, recognising that everyone’s perspective is unique and valuable.
