Our people

Meet the people giving GCBH direction and drive to connect with the wider community. Their diverse set of skills and experiences bring a wealth of ideas and know-how that have and continue to make GCGH a success.

Helen Omondi
CEO
Helen has been the CEO at GCBH for four years. She has worked as a business consultant and has years of experience leading programs in the social services sector, particularly in the areas of health, disability, community and vulnerable people.

Contact: helen@gcbh.com.au

Business Services
