Sports and recreation

 
The sports and recreation program is an active part of Gentown Community & Business Hub. Many of our players got involved through our Employment Skills and Training program. Joining our team has been a great way for these individuals to connect with the community and expand their circle of friends and acquaintances. This program wouldn’t be possible without the incredible support and sponsorships from the following organisations:

 

 

Local Council for providing access to the Gentown Recreation Reserve and club rooms.

Regional Women in Business Network for sponsoring the girl’s AFL and netball teams.

Gentown Lions Club for funding equipment and uniforms for the boys’ soccer club and for sponsoring the Young Achiever Award that promotes youth leadership in sport.
