Our partners

Select each of the headings to learn more about our partners. When you have finished, select again to close the section.

Gentown Chamber of Commerce and Industry (GCCI)

GCCI is a not-for-profit business organisation that liaises with local, state and federal governments on behalf of the Gentown and district business community.

They have representation at the Business & Tourism Regional Alliance (BTRA) association meetings and provide feedback on behalf of Chamber members. GCCI is the local representative body for owners, entrepreneurs, and stakeholders to have their say on the future direction of business in Gentown. They are committed to helping small business start-ups and supporting the Gentown Community & Business Hub achieve this outcome.

Business and Tourism Regional Alliance (BTRA)

BTRA represents local member, Business Tourism Associations and Chambers of Commerce, in the Wiagara Region, who in turn represent their local business and tourism community.

BTRA promotes and supports these communities by identifying opportunities, lobbying relevant authorities, and acting as a co‐coordinating body to facilitate the sharing of information between member organisations across the region.

Gentown Lions Club

Gentown Lions Club is a local group of service-minded volunteers in the Gentown community. Becoming a Lions Club member is by invitation only, however everyone is encouraged to apply.

From supporting the leaders of tomorrow through their youth programs, to rallying together in times of natural disasters, Lions (and their sausage sizzles) are making a huge difference in the Gentown community.

Gentown Arts Centre

Home to more than 20 creative artists, one of the key benefits of having artistic work presented at the Gentown Arts Centre is the encouragement, collaboration, and cross-pollination of ideas that this community inspires. The Gentown Arts Centre supports a vibrant, innovative and active community.

Regional Women in Business Network (RWBN)

RWBN recognises and values the ongoing contributions of women to the business community of the greater Wiagara Region.

In addition to sponsoring an annual Women’s Leadership Gala and Award to celebrate women leaders in the region, RWBN hosts a series of events, like panel discussions with business world experts, that bring to light the various work issues affecting women and men across all sectors.

The network also plays an active role in the local sporting communities by providing opportunities for girls and women to participate in sport.
